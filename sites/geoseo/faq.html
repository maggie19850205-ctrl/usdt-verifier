Frequently Asked Questions

About GEOSEO

What is GEO?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing content to be cited by AI-powered search engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google AI Overview.
Do I need a high-authority site for GEO?
No. GEO favors structured data and clear answers over domain authority. Small sites with proper schema can outperform large sites in AI citations.
What schema types matter most for GEO?
FAQPage, Article, and HowTo schema are the most cited types by AI search engines.
How do I measure GEO success?
Track: citation count in AI search results, referral traffic from AI platforms, and brand mention volume.
