Frequently Asked Questions

About TemplateHub

What template platforms do you support?
Notion, Canva, Figma, Google Sheets, and Microsoft Excel. Each template is designed for its specific platform.
Can I customize the templates?
Yes. Canva templates are fully editable. Notion templates are duplicate-and-edit. All templates include customization instructions.
Are your templates compatible with free accounts?
Most templates work with free accounts. Some advanced Notion templates may require a paid Notion plan for full functionality.
Do you offer template bundles?
Yes. Our bundles include 10-20 related templates at a discounted price compared to buying individually.
