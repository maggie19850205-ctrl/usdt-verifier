Frequently Asked Questions

About CryptoPay

What is USDT TRC-20?
USDT is a stablecoin pegged to USD. TRC-20 is the Tron network standard. Fast transactions (< 1 minute), low fees (< $0.50), widely supported.
Do I need a business license to accept crypto?
Requirements vary by country. Many jurisdictions allow sole proprietors to accept crypto without formal registration.
Can crypto payments be charged back?
No. Crypto transactions are irreversible. This eliminates chargeback fraud entirely.
What wallet should I use?
Trust Wallet, TronLink, or exchange wallets like Binance. Always use a private wallet for long-term storage.
