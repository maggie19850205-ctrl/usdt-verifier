Best Digital Products to Sell Online in 2026 (Low Effort, High Profit)

2026-06-20 AutoMoney Team Digital ProductsEcommercePassive IncomeProduct Ideas

The Digital Product Gold Rush

Digital products are the ultimate passive income vehicle: create once, sell forever. No inventory, no shipping, no perishability. In 2026, the market continues growing as more people work remotely and seek automated solutions.

The key is creating products that people actively search for. Below are categories with highest search demand and lowest competition.

1. Templates and Planners

Notion templates, Canva templates, digital planners cost nothing to duplicate but sell for $5-20 each. Specialized niches (ADHD planners, business dashboards, fitness trackers) have less competition.

Create a bundle of 10-20 related templates and sell for $15-30.

2. Educational Guides and Workbooks

People pay for knowledge packaged as downloadable PDFs. Complete guides on specific topics sell because buyers actively search for solutions.

Best-selling guide topics: AI prompt engineering, side hustle strategies, remote work productivity, crypto basics, digital marketing.

3. AI Prompt Libraries

A category that exploded in 2025-2026. Curated collections of AI prompts for specific use cases. Customers pay $10-30 for tested, reliable collections.

4. Spreadsheets and Dashboards

Google Sheets templates for budgeting, project management, tracking. Sell for $10-25 because they save hours of manual setup.
