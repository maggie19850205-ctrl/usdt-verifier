What Are Digital Products?

Digital products are downloadable or accessible online assets. Common categories include templates (Notion, Canva, PowerPoint), educational content (ebooks, courses, checklists), software tools (apps, browser extensions, calculators), creative assets (fonts, icons, music, stock photos), and printable items (planners, coloring books, worksheets).

Pricing Strategy for Digital Products

Digital products have a unique pricing dynamic: zero marginal cost, but perceived value drives willingness to pay. Research from Gumroad and our own data suggest:

  • Individual tools and templates: $5-15 (our range)
  • Bundles and suites: $20-50
  • Courses and comprehensive guides: $30-100
  • Software with ongoing updates: subscription or $50-200 lifetime
