Case Study

Title: Revolutionizing Revenue with Tiered Pricing: The Case of Zenefits In the realm of SaaS, Zenefits, a comprehensive HR solution provider, implemented a tiered pricing strategy to maximize revenue and cater to a broader clientele base. By offering three distinct plans – Starter, Pro, and Enterprise – Zenefits effectively segmented its market, attracting startups, small businesses, and enterprises respectively. Upon the launch of this strategy, Zenefits witnessed a staggering 180% increase in revenue, alongside a 25% rise in the number of new clients within the first six months. This strategic shift not only boosted the company's revenue but also expanded its customer base, solidifying Zenefits' position as a leading SaaS provider in the HR sector.
