1. **Personalized Content Optimization Tool (PCOT)**
   - Problem: Creating high-quality, optimized content that resonates with the target audience is time-consuming and requires a deep understanding of SEO and audience preferences.
   - Solution: A digital tool that uses AI to analyze a user's content, identifies areas for improvement, and suggests optimizations for SEO, readability, and engagement. It could also provide insights into the best times to post content for maximum reach and engagement.
   - Pricing: Monthly subscription ranging from $3 to $10, depending on the features and volume of content analyzed.

2. **Lead Scoring and Nurturing Platform (LSNP)**
   - Problem: Managing and prioritizing leads can be overwhelming, especially for small businesses. It's difficult to know which leads are most likely to convert.
   - Solution: A digital platform that uses AI to score leads based on their engagement with marketing materials, website behavior, and other factors. It could also provide automated, personalized nurturing sequences to move leads through the sales funnel.
   - Pricing: Monthly subscription ranging from $3 to $10, depending on the number of leads and automation features.
