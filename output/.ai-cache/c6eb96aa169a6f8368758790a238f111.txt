Here is a description of 5 specific templates for a **"Logo Design Inspiration Pack"**, designed to help designers, entrepreneurs, and agencies kickstart their branding process.

Each template is a structured digital file (e.g., Figma, Illustrator, or Canva) that combines visual examples with strategic prompts.

---

### Template 1: The "Mood & Archetype" Starter Board

**What it contains:**
- **12 pre-filled mood boxes** (e.g., "Minimalist," "Vintage," "Playful," "Luxury").
- **A drop-down menu for Brand Archetypes** (e.g., Hero, Outlaw, Caregiver).
- **A color swatch library** with 5 recommended palettes per archetype.
- **3 placeholder logo silhouettes** (abstract, lettermark, icon) to test visual weight.

**How to use it:**
1.  Select the brand’s core archetype from the drop-down.
2.  Drag and drop the brand’s existing colors or choose from the recommended palettes.
3.  Place the 3 silhouette placeholders over the mood boxes to see which shape language fits the vibe.
4.  Use the final board as a visual brief for the designer.
