Here is a description of a **5-template Canva Template Bundle**, designed for a creative entrepreneur, coach, or small business owner. Each template is fully customizable in Canva (Free or Pro).

---

### Bundle Name: "The Creator's Toolkit"

**Overall Theme:** Modern, minimalist, and high-conversion. Designed to help users build a cohesive brand presence from lead generation to sale.

---

### 1. The "Lead Magnet" Opt-In Page

**What it contains:**
- A single-page, mobile-responsive document (1080x1920 px).
- **Sections:** Headline, subheadline, bulleted list of 3-5 benefits, a mock "email sign-up" form (with placeholder text), a hero image placeholder, and a "Call to Action" button.
- **Design elements:** A clean white background, two brand accent colors, soft drop shadows, and a sans-serif font pairing (e.g., Montserrat + Lora).

**How to use it:**
1.  **Replace the image:** Drag and drop a high-quality photo or illustration into the image frame.
2.  **Edit the text:** Click on the headline and subheadline to describe your freebie (e.g., "The 5-Day Productivity Guide").
3.  **Customize the button:** Change the button text to "Get Instant Access" or "Download Now". Adjust the button color to match your brand.
4.  **Export:** Download as a PNG or JPG to use as a landing page graphic for your email service provider (e.g., ConvertKit, Mailchimp) or as an Instagram story cover.
