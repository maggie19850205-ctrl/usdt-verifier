Core Template Frameworks

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my marketing video be?

It depends on the platform. For social media, keep it under 60 seconds. For YouTube, 8-12 minutes is optimal for algorithmic promotion.

Do I need expensive equipment?

No. Modern smartphones shoot 4K video. Use a $20 ring light and free editing software like DaVinci Resolve for professional results.

How do I write a compelling hook?

Start with a relatable pain point or surprising statistic in the first 3 seconds. Example: 'Did you know 80% of viewers stop watching after 10 seconds?'
