Color Palette Collection - Complete Guide

Understanding Color Harmony

Use complementary, analogous, or triadic schemes. Complementary colors (opposite on the wheel) create contrast; analogous (adjacent) offer harmony. Choose based on the mood you want to evoke.

Psychology of Color

Colors evoke emotions: blue for trust, green for growth, red for urgency. Align your palette with your brand's personality. For example, a wellness brand might use earth tones.

Practical Application

Start with one primary color, then build. Use the 60-30-10 rule: 60% neutral, 30% secondary, 10% accent. Test on different screens and in print to ensure consistency.
